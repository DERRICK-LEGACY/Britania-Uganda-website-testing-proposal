Manufacturer

KAMPALA- The sky-rocketing demand for beverages by the populace has compelled Britania Allied Industries Ltd to rethink customer-driven market trends about their product lines. On Friday, August 2, they launched “Splash Juice Kachupa” in PET bottles at their premises in Ntinda. With a tagline “Sip it Keep it”, the product will be in three flavours that include apple, mango, and orange.
