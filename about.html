Our Story

We are the largest food and beverage manufacturer in East Africa. Dr Hasmukh Dawda set up BAIL in 1991 and at the time it was called Britania Products (U) Ltd, originally producing only biscuits. In 1994, another company called Britania Foods was formed to produce Splash juice and tomato sauce. They were operating as separate entities until 2002 when we merged them to form the present-day BAIL.

*ABOUT BRITANIA ALLIED INDUSTRIES LIMITED (BAIL)*

The Britania biscuit manufacturing in Kampala, was set up in 1991 by the House of Dawda group – a group which has been in existence since 1962 in the East African region – pioneered by Dr Hasmukh Dawda. Britania Allied Industries Ltd. (BAIL) was created through the merger of 2 operating subsidiaries. We are in the business of manufacturing Biscuits; Confectionery; Fruit Juices and Sauces. Our production facilities are among the most sophisticated and modern in the region and we possess a level of technological excellence compared to prevailing global standards. We have highly reliable process equipment and methods in all our production facilities – Tetra Pak for fruit juices and auto filling machines are a few to mention. Late 2002, the company acquired the present Manji Food Industries Ltd., biscuit factory at Nairobi. Britania owes its success to its creative, determined, dependable and enterprising people and the co-operation of an extensive network of coperation

*What does Britania Allied Industries produce?*

We have a wide range of premium-quality products comprising delicious fruit juices, tasty biscuits, rich sauces, and flavorful fruit-flavoured syrups. Splash, our flagship brand, stands out as a pure, refreshing fruit juice made from carefully selected, high-quality tropical fruits sourced and grown in Uganda. Each product is crafted to deliver unmatched taste, nutrition, and satisfaction, making Britania Allied Industries a trusted name in households across the region.
